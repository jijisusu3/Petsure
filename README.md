- ### Project Outline

  - Link : [PetSure](https://j7b202.p.ssafy.io/)

  - `PetSure` is a pet insurance recommendation and comparison web site that recommends the most suitable insurance product to users based on the SURE index developed using big data. We provide users a simpler way to compare pet insurance and understand the information, avoiding complicated clauses.

- ### Main Functionality and Service

#### - 1. Basic Search\*\*

> 1. This function is for user who want to take a quick look.
>
> 2. Users can recieve recommended insurance information by entering their **pet's name, species, and birth date**
>
> 3. The basic search feature does not provide a SURE index, insurance comparison, or insurance coverage calculator.

#### - **2. Detail Search**

> 1. Users can recieve recommended insurance information by entering coverage preference survey with **8 simple questions, pet's name, species, and birth date**.
> 2. At this time, the recommended insurance is sorted in one of the following order: SURE Index, Lowest Price, Most Coverage. The sorting method can be selected by the user.
> 3. The best 3 insurances sorted are displayed as Top 3 cards in the top section.

#### - **3. Insurance Product Comparison**

> 1. Easily compare up to three insurance products.
>
> 2. This includes eight types of coverage: outpatient treatment expenses, hospitalization treatment expenses, surgical treatment expenses, knee joint treatments, skin diseases, oral health, urology, and liability coverage. Additionally, the feature provides a Sure Index graph and a coverage amount calculator.
>
> 3. Coverage amount calculator: Make easier it for consumers to know how much the product actually covers using information such as coverage amount limit and deductible terms.

####

**[Insurance Product Comparison Feature]:** The user can compare up to 3 insurance products, and within the comparison feature, you can also use the coverage amount calculator to compare coverage amounts.

#### - **4. Insurance Detail**

> The insurance detail page includes the following elements when the user clicks on specific insurance's detail button after detail searching.
>
> 1. **SURE Index** : SURE Index is calculated based on the company trust rate, relief coverage score, and expected suitability.
> 2. **Focused coverage product graph** : Users can check which product is focused on coverage among surgical treatment costs, urinary, oral, skin, and knee joints in the pentagonal graph.
> 3. **Coverage details information** : View the details of coverage provided through basic clauses and exclusion clauses.
> 4. **User's feedback graph** : A user can visually see the other user's feedback results.
> 5. **Survey** : Rate satisfaction with insurance product.
> 6. **Recommend alternative products** : Suggest pet products that can substitute for items not coverded by relevant insurance policies but helped for pet's health based on their species and age.
> 7. **Coverage amount calculator**: Make easier it for consumers to know how much the product actually covers using information such as coverage amount limit and deductible terms.
